FunGro

FunGro is a mobile app for iOS created to help high school students practice functional groups. This project won an award at ITESM's engineering project expo.

Background

The idea came after conducting surveys with students from a local high school. The students indicated that they had more difficulty with functional groups and that mobile devices were the most common when studying.
